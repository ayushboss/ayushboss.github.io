This portfolio includes many of the programming projects I've worked on over the years. You can click on projects to view more information.

Inquis

Mobile Application developed to improve communication between patients on the autism spectrum, their doctors, and researchers. Won first prize at the 2018 SPARK for Autism Mobile Application Contest and reached state-wide judging in the Diamond Challenge. Now available on the Google Play Store.







The Evaluation and Quantification of the Performance of Stream Ciphers

Research project at Texas State University from July 2019 to December 2020 with Professor Dan Tamir. Compared the performance of different pseudorandom number generators based on the NIST SP 800-22. Publication currently pending.








Latin Resources

Android and iOS Application designed during Summer 2016. Developed to assist Latin students with preparing for Junior Classical League tournaments.








Houston Helps

Web Application developed at the 2017 Houston Hackathon. Linked members of the Houston Refugee community with pre-screened CraigsList donors to help obtain basic essentials.
